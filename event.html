Getting In

Because this a special event, please do not enter the aquarium through the main entrance. When facing the Pier 3 Main Aquarium Building, walk down the brick pier on the right hand side of building past the main entry doors located in the new Crystal Pavilion. Go past the Lightship Chesapeake. You will enter through the office entry sliding glass doors across from the gangplank of the Submarine Torsk. Tell the folks at the desk that you're here for the wedding and you're golden.

If you would like to check out the exhibits before the ceremony, you can arrive starting at 7pm for a complimentary look-see.

Cookie Table

We may not be in Pittsburgh, but we're bringing Pittsburgh to us. We will be having a cookie table at the reception. If you're bringing cookies, you'll be able to hand them off to catering once you get to the aquarium.
